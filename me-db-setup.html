SI Units Concept important

Original values are always preserved. Converted SI values are additionally stored:

ColumnWhereMeaning
measurements.unitmeasurementsOriginal unit of this measurement (e.g. mg/l for Russian lab)
measurements.value_simeasurementsValue converted to international unit
parameters.unit_siparametersInternational target unit (e.g. mmol/l for magnesium)
parameters.ref_range_low/high_siparametersReference range in SI unit
Example magnesium: Russia measures in mg/l (20.98 mg/l = 0.863 mmol/l), Germany in mmol/l (0.93 mmol/l). The app always shows mmol/l in the dashboard and trend.

Row Level Security (RLS)

lab_reports, staged_measurements, documents, chat_* and the storage bucket have RLS policies (anon + authenticated). parameters and measurements have RLS disabled — the anon key can read/write/delete directly here (single-user setup), which is why full edit/delete works in the frontend.
